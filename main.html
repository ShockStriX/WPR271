Fitness Tips & Exercise Guides

Strength Training Basics

Progressive Overload: Gradually increase weight, reps, or sets each week.

  • Start with compound movements
  • Focus on proper form first
  • Rest 2-3 minutes between sets

Cardio Training Guide

HIIT vs LISS: Mix both high and low intensity cardio for optimal results.

  • HIIT: 15-20 minutes, 3x per week
  • LISS: 30-45 minutes, 2-3x per week
  • Target heart rate: 70-85% max HR

Nutrition Fundamentals

Macronutrient Balance: Fuel your workouts with proper nutrition timing.

  • Protein: 0.8-1g per lb bodyweight
  • Pre-workout: Carbs + light protein
  • Stay hydrated: 8-10 glasses daily

Recovery & Sleep

Rest is Growth: Your muscles grow during recovery, not just workouts.

  • Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep
  • Take 1-2 complete rest days per week
  • Include stretching and foam rolling
